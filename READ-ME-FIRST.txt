IntGen Stay Intelligence Engine
SUNWAY IntGen · Hospitality AI Platform
========================================

WHAT THIS IS
------------
One landing page that opens into three standalone intelligence products:

  1. Intelligent Demand Forecasting  — lobby & venue occupancy, demand forecasts, staffing
  2. AI Training Platform            — hotel staff training, SOPs, assignments
  3. AI Security Command Center      — live security/operations situational map

The landing page also features three videos (aerial hero, suite, and an F&B service band).


HOW TO OPEN
-----------
Easiest (recommended — guarantees the videos autoplay):

  • Mac:      double-click  "Start (Mac).command"
  • Windows:  double-click  "Start (Windows).bat"

  This starts a tiny local server and opens the site in your browser.
  Keep the little black window open while viewing; close it when done.

Quick look (no server):

  • Double-click  index.html
    Works in most browsers. If a background video doesn't play, use Safari,
    or use the "Start" launcher above.


NOTES
-----
• 100% offline / self-contained. No installation, no internet required
  (web fonts load online if available, otherwise system fonts are used).
• From the landing page, click any of the three product cards to enter that app.
• Use your browser's Back button to return to the landing page.
